Ejemplo: <paramSpec>

These search results reproduce every example of the use of <paramSpec> in the Guidelines, including all localised and translated versions. In some cases, the examples have been drawn from discussion of other elements in the Guidelines and illustrating the use of <paramSpec> is not the main focus of the passage in question. In other cases, examples may be direct translations of each other, and hence identical from the perspective of their encoding.

22.5.4.8 Defining a processing model

<valItem ident="link">
 <desc>create a hyperlink</desc>
 <paramList>
  <paramSpec ident="content">
   <desc>supplies the location of some content describing the link</desc>
  </paramSpec>
  <paramSpec ident="uri">
   <desc>supplies the location of the intended hyperlink</desc>
  </paramSpec>
 </paramList>
</valItem>
