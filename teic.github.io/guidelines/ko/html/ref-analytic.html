<analytic>

<analytic> (분석적 층위) 독립 출판이 아닌, 단행본 또는 학술지 내에 포함되어 출판된 항목(예를 들어, 논문 또는 시)을 기술하는 참고문헌 요소를 포함한다. [3.11.2.1 Analytic, Monographic, and Series Levels]
모듈core — Elements Available in All TEI Documents
속성att.global (@xml:id, @n, @xml:lang, @xml:base, @xml:space) (att.global.rendition (@rend, @style, @rendition)) (att.global.linking (@corresp, @synch, @sameAs, @copyOf, @next, @prev, @exclude, @select)) (att.global.analytic (@ana)) (att.global.facs (@facs)) (att.global.change (@change)) (att.global.responsibility (@cert, @resp)) (att.global.source (@source))
에 의해 포함된
core: biblStruct
포함할 수 있다
core: author date editor ptr ref respStmt textLang title
header: availability idno
주석

May contain titles and statements of responsibility (author, editor, or other), in any order.

The analytic element may only occur within a biblStruct, where its use is mandatory for the description of an analytic level bibliographic item.

예
<biblStruct>
 <analytic>
  <author>Chesnutt, David</author>
  <title>Historical Editions in the States</title>
 </analytic>
 <monogr>
  <title level="j">Computers and the Humanities</title>
  <imprint>
   <date when="1991-12">(December, 1991):</date>
  </imprint>
  <biblScope>25.6</biblScope>
  <biblScope>377–380</biblScope>
 </monogr>
</biblStruct>
