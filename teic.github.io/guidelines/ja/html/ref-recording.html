<recording>

<recording> (recording event) 発話テキストの元資料として使われる，直接または放送から事象を録音，録 画したものの詳細を示す． [8.2 Documenting the Source of Transcribed Speech 15.3.2 Declarable Elements]
モジュールspoken — Transcriptions of Speech
属性att.global (@xml:id, @n, @xml:lang, @xml:base, @xml:space) (att.global.rendition (@rend, @style, @rendition)) (att.global.linking (@corresp, @synch, @sameAs, @copyOf, @next, @prev, @exclude, @select)) (att.global.analytic (@ana)) (att.global.facs (@facs)) (att.global.change (@change)) (att.global.responsibility (@cert, @resp)) (att.global.source (@source)) att.declarable (@default) att.duration (att.duration.w3c (@dur)) (att.duration.iso (@dur-iso)) att.typed (type, @subtype)
type type¶録音，録画の種類．
Derived fromatt.typed
状態 任意
データ型 teidata.enumerated
正当な値:
audio
録音． [初期値]
video
録音，録画．
上位
spoken: broadcast recordingStmt
下位
core: date media p respStmt time
linking: ab
spoken: broadcast equipment
解説

属性durは，当該録音，録画の，元の長さを示すために使用さ れる．

例
<recording type="audio" dur="P30M">
 <equipment>
  <p>Recorded on a Sony TR444 walkman by unknown participants; remastered
     to digital tape at <placeName>Borehamwood Studios</placeName> by
  <orgName>Transcription Services Inc</orgName>.</p>
 </equipment>
</recording>
一括表示 
例
<recording type="audio" dur="P10M">

  <p>Recorded from FM Radio to digital tape</p>
 </equipment>

   <title>Interview on foreign policy</title>

    <resp>interviewer</resp>
    <name>Robin Day</name>
   </respStmt>

    <resp>interviewee</resp>
    <name>Margaret Thatcher</name>
   </respStmt>
   <series>
    <title>The World Tonight</title>
   </series>
   <note>First broadcast on
   <date when="1989-11-27">27 Nov 89</date>
   </note>
  </bibl>
 </broadcast>
</recording>
