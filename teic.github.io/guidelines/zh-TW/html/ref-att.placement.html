att.placement

att.placement 提供屬性，描述文件元素在來源頁面或物件上出現的位置。 [3.4.3 Additions, Deletions, and Omissions 11.3.1.4 Additions and Deletions]

within the body of the text.
inspace
in a predefined space, for example left by an earlier scribe.
